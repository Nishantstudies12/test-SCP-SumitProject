Welcome To Edu Manager, an E-Learning Platform

Using our platform, every institute can now run online classes!

First of Institute needs to signup, add teachers and create different rooms for different class/batch of students and then add all courses.

Added teachers can now start uploading content to assigned courses.

Unique link for each room can be given to students to join just like Google Classroom.

All other features are self explanatory and easy to use.
